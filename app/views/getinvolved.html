Get Involved

While we are made up of members, the group is still very much in its infancy compared to related meetups in OC. We need all kinds of help:

  • Presenters to talk about AngularJS, how they're using it in real life, lessons learned from migrating existing code, etc.
  • Sponsors who can extend job and networking opportunities, help with the cost of food, raffle prizes, and cool schwag
  • Hosts who can provide us with a fun meeting space to socialize and learn
  • Volunteers who can film presentations, take pictures, promote, find speakers or help out in any way
