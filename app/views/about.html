About

The AngularJS OC meetup was started in July 2013 to serve AngularJS enthusiasts in Orange County. At the time there was already a group that met in Los Angeles. Southern Californians know that might as well be Canada with the distance and traffic separating our geographies.

The explosion of MVC frameworks in the web community might lead to analysis paralysis. Make your life simple by reducing your choice to just AngularJS. Organize your code and iterate faster. Learn what some are already calling "HTML6".

The awesomeness of our young meetup is due to volunteers who lend their time and talent. Can you help?

Organizer: Loc Nguyen
