About

The AngularJS-OC meetup was started in July 2013 to serve AngularJS enthusiasts in the Orange County area. At the time there was already a group that met in Los Angeles. Southern Californians know that might as well be Canada with the distance and traffic separating our geographies.

While we are made up of members, the group is still very much in its infancy compared to related meetups in OC. We need all kinds of help:

  • Presenters to talk about AngularJS, how they're using it in real life, lessons learned from migrating existing code, etc.
  • Sponsors who can extend job and networking opportunities, help with the cost of food, and provide raffle prizes
  • Hosts who can provide us with a fun meeting space
